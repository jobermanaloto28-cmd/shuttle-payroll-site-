Privacy Policy — Shuttle Payroll Automation

Last updated: July 7, 2026

This application ("the App") is an internal payroll automation tool used solely by K&M Automation for processing driver payroll for its shuttle transport operations.

Data We Access

The App connects to Google Drive, Google Docs, and Google Sheets APIs to:

  • Read driver and route rate information from a private Google Sheets spreadsheet
  • Create and edit a copy of a payslip template stored in Google Docs
  • Export the completed payslip as a PDF file

Data We Do Not Do

  • We do not share, sell, or transfer any accessed data to third parties
  • We do not use accessed data for advertising purposes
  • We do not access any files, sheets, or documents beyond those explicitly used for payroll processing

Data Storage

Payroll records are stored in a private Google Sheets spreadsheet owned and controlled by K&M Automation. Generated payslips are stored in Google Drive under the same account.

Scope of Use

This App is used exclusively by the developer/administrator of this payroll system and is not distributed publicly. Access is limited to authorized personnel of K&M Automation.
